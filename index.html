Meet the Founder

A commitment to tradition and health.

Our founder holds a deep passion for traditional Indian nutrition. Recognizing the declining use of ancient grains in modern diets, they set out to create Bheema Foods. The mission is simple: to make heritage foods accessible, convenient, and delicious for the modern family without compromising on purity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order?

Add your favorite items to the cart and click "Order via WhatsApp". It's that simple! We will confirm your order and shipping details directly.

Are there any preservatives?

No, all our products are 100% free from preservatives, artificial colors, and synthetic flavors. We rely on careful roasting and premium ingredients for shelf life.

How long do the mixes last?

Unopened, they last for 6 months. Once opened, we recommend transferring them to an airtight container and consuming within 30-45 days.
