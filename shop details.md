# Pricing Strategy

Bheema Foods should position itself as:

**"Affordable Premium Traditional Nutrition"**

Not cheap, not luxury — but trusted heritage health food.

Use psychological pricing:

* ₹169
* ₹179
* ₹189
* ₹199

Instead of round numbers like ₹170 or ₹200.

# Branding Structure (Important)

Each product should have two layers:

**Customer Friendly Name**
Example: Karuppu Kavuni Strength Mix

**Brand Story Title**

This keeps the website modern while preserving the royal brand identity.
